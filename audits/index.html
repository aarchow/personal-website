Remote Codebase Audits

I'm available for hire for remote codebase audits for React applications. The benefit is twofold: of course a remote audit will leave you with a better codebase, but the much bigger benefit lies in the improvement of your day-to-day work. Essentially, this is a lesson tailored around your codebase and the way you and/or your team work which will prevent you from mistakes and pitfalls in the future.

These audits on a specific form:

  1. I get access to your codebase and spend a day deeply investigating and auditing it
  2. I compile a document with recommendations and suggestions to improve the code and tooling
  3. After you've received and digested the document we have an hour long chat about what I uncovered and to answer any questions you may have

These code audits have a fixed price of 1,499€ (including tax), half of which is paid upfront.

If this sounds like something you might want to do or if you have any questions please reach out to me at contact@mxstbr.com!

Who am I?

In case you're new here, a short introduction to myself:

I'm a speaker, sharing my knowledge on stages in front of thousands of people. I've had the opportunity to talk about React and related topics at more than a dozen conferences all across the world!
